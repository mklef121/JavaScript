Nigeria's Education Sector Overview

Early Childhood Education (ECCDE)

Only 1 in 3 children attends ECCDE, highlighting significant disparities between urban and rural areas.

Primary Education Enrollment

Enrollment shows gender parity, but with regional disparities. The GAR improved from 75% to 90%.

Junior Secondary Education

60% of junior secondary schools are private, with 80% of eligible children attending some form of education.

Senior Secondary Education

Attendance is higher among urban boys, with only 33% of students in the correct educational level for their age.
