Understanding Nigeria's Education System

Introduction

Nigeria's education system is multifaceted, with significant disparities in access and quality across different regions and socio-economic groups. Below are graphical representations to better understand these dynamics.

Pre-Primary Education

Only 1 in 3 children have access to ECCDE programs, with urban areas significantly outpacing rural areas.

Primary Education

Enrollment is high, but gender and regional disparities persist, particularly in the North.

Secondary Education

Enrollment drops as students move from junior to senior secondary, with notable urban-rural differences.
