Nigeria's Informal Economy Insights

Age Distribution in the Informal Sector

Over half of the workforce in Nigeria's informal economy is under 34 years old.

Gender Participation

Women constitute 37.1% of the informal sector, highlighting significant but unequal participation.

Regional Contribution

The South-West leads with 30% of the informal economy activities.

Industry Focus

Retail and General Trade dominate, representing 24% of the sector.
