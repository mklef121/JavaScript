Nigerian Agriculture Sector Overview

GDP Contribution

Agriculture contributes between 24.45% to 25.70% to Nigeria's GDP.

Household Food Insecurity and Malnutrition

38.6% face food insecurity, 28.7% nutrition insecurity.

Agricultural Productivity

Key Crops:

  • Rice: Average yield 2.5 tons/ha
  • Maize: Production gap of 5 million MT
  • Wheat: Target to increase from 3.0 to 5.0 T/ha
  • Cassava: World's largest producer at 61.121 million metric tons

Livestock

Cattle population: 20 million

Fisheries and Aquaculture

Fish deficit: 2.4 million tons
