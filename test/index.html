Nigeria GDP Report Q4 2023

Summary

Quarterly GDP Growth: 3.46% in Q4 2023, compared to 3.52% in Q4 2022 and 2.54% in Q3 2023.

Annual GDP Growth: 2.74% in 2023, down from 3.10% in 2022.

Aggregate GDP (Nominal): N65,908,258.59 million in Q4 2023, up from N56,757,889.95 million in Q4 2022.

Oil Sector: Real growth of 12.11% in Q4 2023. Annual growth was -2.22% in 2023.

Non-Oil Sector: Grew by 3.07% in Q4 2023. Annual growth was 3.04% in 2023.
