Nigeria Education Sector Analysis

Pre-Primary Education Enrollment

In 2018, over 7 million children attended pre-primary education. Gender disparity is notable, with fewer girls attending in the North East and North West zones.

Primary Education Enrollment

Primary education shows significant gender disparities, especially in the North West zone. The age discrepancy highlights the challenge of under/overage schooling.

Junior Secondary Education Enrollment

Approximately 80% of eligible children attended junior secondary schools, with a large portion enrolled in private institutions, reflecting their critical role in the education sector.

Senior Secondary Attendance

Senior secondary attendance rates vary significantly by location and gender. Urban boys have higher attendance compared to rural girls, with considerable variation in gross and net attendance rates.

Data Source: UNESCO, Multiple Indicator Cluster Survey (2017), Nigeria Living Standards Survey (2018/2019)
