Nigeria GDP Report Q4 2023

Nigeria's GDP grew by 3.46% in Q4 2023, a slight decrease from 3.52% in Q4 2022 but an increase from 2.54% in Q3 2023. The Services sector was the main driver, growing by 3.98% and contributing 56.55% to GDP. The Agriculture sector saw a modest growth of 2.10%, while Industry improved significantly to 3.86% from -0.94%. Oil production increased to 1.55 mbpd, with the sector growing by 12.11% in real terms. However, the non-oil sector, which grew by 3.07%, remains the backbone of the economy, contributing 95.30% to GDP.
