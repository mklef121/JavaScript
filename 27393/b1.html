Nigeria GDP Report Summary - Q4 2023

Nigeria's GDP grew by 3.46% in Q4 2023, with the Services sector leading at 3.98% growth. Despite a slight decrease from the previous year, the overall annual growth for 2023 was 2.74%. The oil sector saw significant improvement with a 12.11% real growth year-on-year, while the non-oil sector grew by 3.07%.
